Welcome, Tarnished

The Roundtable Hold is a sanctuary for Tarnished, but it cannot be found wandering the Lands Between normally. It can only be accessed through fast travel after Melina extends her invitation. This page acts as a guide and covers all the methods to unlock and reach this mysterious hub.

Key Requirements

Location Unlock

You must progress to a point where Melina offers to take you to the Roundtable Hold.

Early Progression

The location is designed to be accessible early in your playthrough.

Melina's Guidance

Melina, your guide, must officially invite you and take you to the Hold.

Fast Travel

Once unlocked, access it through the Map using fast travel.

Methods to Unlock Roundtable Hold

Method 1: Challenge Margit, the Fell Omen

⭐ Easiest - Recommended

The most straightforward approach. Simply attempt Margit, the Fell Omen boss fight - you don't even need to defeat him. After resting at a site of grace, Melina will appear with an invitation.

Steps:

  1. Travel to Stormveil Castle in Limgrave
  2. Progress through the castle to find Margit, the Fell Omen
  3. Attempt the boss fight (losing is fine!)
  4. Rest at any Site of Grace
  5. Melina will appear and offer to take you to Roundtable Hold

Why This Works:

Encountering a Shard Bearer serves as a trigger for Melina to invite you to the Hold.

Pro Tip: This can be done as early as Limgrave and requires minimal progression.

Method 2: Lake-Facing Cliffs in Liurnia

⭐ Easy - Early Game

Reach Liurnia of the Lakes and rest at the Lake-Facing Cliffs Site of Grace. Melina will appear to invite you.

Steps:

  1. Exit Limgrave toward the north
  2. Navigate past Stormhill to find a broken bridge
  3. Go down the path (wolves will guard it)
  4. Reach Liurnia of the Lakes
  5. Rest at Lake-Facing Cliffs Site of Grace
  6. Melina will invite you to Roundtable Hold

Map Markers:

The path starts north of Stormhill Shack and navigates around wolves to reach Liurnia early before defeating major bosses.

Warning: This sequence break may affect Roderika's quest progression.

Method 3: Smoldering Church in Caelid

⭐⭐ Moderate - Mid Game

Reach Caelid and find the Smoldering Church. Rest at the Site of Grace inside to receive Melina's invitation.

Steps:

  1. Travel north from Stormhill Shack
  2. Pass Saintsbridge and Summonwater Village
  3. Enter Caelid
  4. Find the Smoldering Church on the left side
  5. Rest at the Site of Grace inside
  6. Melina will invite you

Notable:

The church entrance is guarded by Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater, an NPC invader. You can rush past her or engage in combat.

Tip: You can force quit if defeated to respawn inside, or wait for Anastasia to kill you and respawn at the grace.

Method 4: Bestial Sanctum (Teleporter Route)

⭐⭐ Moderate - Sequence Break

Use the teleporter near the Third Church of Marika to reach Dragonbarrow and the Bestial Sanctum. This is a fast sequence break method.

Steps:

  1. Locate the Third Church of Marika in eastern Limgrave
  2. Find the way-gate (teleporter) just north of the church
  3. Activate the teleporter to reach Dragonbarrow
  4. Navigate to Bestial Sanctum
  5. Rest at the Site of Grace inside
  6. Melina will invite you

Location Details:

The Bestial Sanctum is where you meet Gurranq, Beast Clergyman. This area is in Dragonbarrow, the northern region of Caelid.

Speedrun Route: This is popular for sequence breaks and speedruns due to its early accessibility.

Method 5: Sellia Under-Stair in Caelid

⭐⭐⭐ Challenging - Complex Route

Navigate through a complex sequence involving a trapped chest teleport to reach Sellia. This method is more roundabout but viable.

Steps:

  1. Locate Dragon-Burnt Ruins in Limgrave
  2. Open the trapped chest inside (it teleports you)
  3. You'll appear at Sellia Crystal Tunnel in Caelid
  4. Escape the tunnel
  5. Find Sellia Under-Stair Site of Grace
  6. Rest there for Melina's invitation

Important Notes:

The teleportation from the trapped chest is automatic and unavoidable. You'll have a temporary debuff upon arrival.

Recommendation: Rest at the grace immediately upon arrival to remove the debuff and trigger Melina's invitation.

Method 6: Siofra River Well Exit

⭐⭐ Moderate - Underground Route

Navigate through Siofra River and exit via the elevator at the end. You'll emerge in Caelid with a Site of Grace nearby.

Steps:

  1. Find the entrance to Siofra River Well in Limgrave
  2. Navigate through the underground area
  3. Reach the elevator at the very end
  4. Ascend to emerge in Caelid
  5. Rest at the nearby Site of Grace
  6. Melina appears with her invitation

Surroundings:

When you exit, you'll be near the Great Jar and surrounded by branching roads. One leads to the Swamp of Aeonia (dangerous), the other to safer areas.

Exploration: This method is good if you're already exploring the underground regions.

After You Unlock Roundtable Hold

Accessing the Hold Going Forward

Once Melina takes you to the Roundtable Hold for the first time, you can access it anytime through:

Step 1: Open Your Map

Press the map button (Tab on PC, Menu buttons on console)

Step 2: Look at Bottom Left

The Roundtable Hold appears as a fast travel point in the bottom left corner of the map

Step 3: Select and Fast Travel

Click on the Roundtable Hold grace icon and fast travel there instantly

Safety and Pact

Combat is prohibited within the Roundtable Hold due to a pact of non-aggression made between all Tarnished. The icon at the top of your screen (Roundtable symbol) indicates whether you're protected by this pact.

Progression Timeline

Early Game (1-20 Hours)

Visit to meet NPCs, upgrade initial weapons, and gather information

Mid Game (20-60 Hours)

Regular visits for weapon upgrades, merchant shopping, and quest progressions

Late Game (60+ Hours)

NPCs may leave or their status changes; the Hold becomes emptier as game progresses

End Game (After Erdtree Burns)

The Hold itself begins to burn; most NPCs leave or meet tragic ends

Tips & Tricks

Fastest Method

Challenging Margit is the quickest and most reliable method available to new players.

Speedrun Route

The Bestial Sanctum teleporter method bypasses most early content for sequence breaks.

Multiple Visits

Visit frequently - NPCs change locations, items respawn, and questlines progress.

Bell Bearings

Collect Bell Bearings throughout your journey to expand merchant inventory at the Hold.

Map Corner

Once unlocked, the Hold is always in the bottom-left corner of your map for quick reference.

Safe Haven

Use it as a respite - no enemies, no combat, just preparation and shopping.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I access Roundtable Hold without meeting Melina first?

No, Melina must personally invite you. She appears at certain sites of grace after meeting specific conditions.

What if I refuse Melina's invitation?

You can decline and she'll offer again at different sites of grace. You can't permanently refuse.

Is there combat in Roundtable Hold?

Generally no - it's a protected space. However, specific encounters (like Mad Tongue Alberich) are exceptions.

Can I return to Roundtable Hold after leaving?

Yes! Use fast travel from your map anytime. Once unlocked, it's permanently accessible.

What's the earliest I can access it?

Via the Bestial Sanctum teleporter, you can access it before defeating any major bosses.

Why do NPCs leave the Hold?

As their questlines progress, NPCs travel elsewhere in the world. Their story arcs lead them away.
