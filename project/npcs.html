Gideon Ofnir

The All-Knowing Leader & Counselor

The senior member and leader of the Roundtable Hold. Gideon is described as the All-Knowing, a warrior of immense wisdom and knowledge about the Shardbearers.

Services:

  • Provides information about Shard Bearers and their locations
  • Offers strategic counsel and guidance
  • Shares lore and secrets of the Lands Between

Location:

Sitting room - his private chamber at the Roundtable Hold

Note:

Becoming Elden Lord is his ultimate goal and he will "support" your journey. Until he doesn't...

Smithing Master Hewg

Blacksmith & Craftsman Weapon Upgrades

A master blacksmith dedicated to his craft. Hewg is bound to the Roundtable Hold by circumstances unknown to him, yet he remains committed to enhancing warriors' armaments.

Services:

  • Weapon upgrading (standard weapons up to +25 and unique weapons to +10)
  • Special weapon enhancement
  • Affinity adjustment
  • Requires: Smithing Stones & Somber Smithing Stones

Location:

Forge chamber - equipped with anvil and equipment

Special Note:

Hewg's dialogue hints at a mysterious past and purpose within the Hold.

Twin Maiden Husks

Merchant Vendors General & Specialty Goods

Enigmatic merchant NPCs who manage the Roundtable's shop. They accept Bell Bearings to expand their inventory and offer a wide variety of goods.

Services:

  • Crafting materials and upgrade components
  • Consumables and restorative items
  • Armor and weapons (with Bell Bearings)
  • Ammunition and tools

Location:

Shop chamber - main trading area

Bell Bearing System:

Bring them Bell Bearings found throughout the Lands Between to unlock new inventory items.

Roderika

Spirit Tuner Spirit Ash Enhancement

Once a wanderer at Stormhill Shack, Roderika has found her purpose as a Spirit Tuner at the Roundtable. She enhances and attunes Spirit Ashes for warriors.

Services:

  • Spirit Ash enhancement and leveling
  • Spirit Ash information and guidance
  • Compatibility advice

Location:

Spirit Ash chamber - with enhancement tools

Quest Connection:

Part of a larger questline involving her journey to find purpose.

Brother Corhyn

Holy Cleric Incantation Merchant

A devoted cleric of the Golden Order who sells sacred incantations. He shares knowledge of holy magic and faith-based powers.

Services:

  • Incantation spells - holy and sacred magic
  • Sacred seal recommendations
  • Faith-based guidance

Location:

Prayer chamber - dedicated to holy contemplation

Note:

Leave if his questline progresses elsewhere in the world.

Sorcerer Rogier

Spellcaster Sorcery Practitioner

A talented Tarnished sorcerer first encountered at Stormveil Castle. He practices advanced sorcery and serves as a fellow warrior within the Hold.

Services:

  • Sorcery spells and knowledge
  • Magical theory discussion
  • Quest assistance

Location:

Study area - researching sorcery

Character Arc:

His questline has significant story implications related to the Dead.

Nepheli Loux

Warrior Tarnished Companion

A skilled Tarnished warrior first met at Stormveil Castle. She seeks to become Elden Lord and values honesty and strength in her allies.

Services:

  • Summon for certain boss fights
  • Quest assistance
  • Warrior companionship and support

Location:

Common area - resting or preparing for battle

Character Development:

Her questline explores themes of legacy and inheritance.

D, Hunter of the Dead

Undead Hunter Guide & Protector

A mysterious hunter dedicated to eliminating undead abominations. He offers cryptic guidance about the Dead and those who practice death magic.

Services:

  • Information about Gurranq, Beast Clergyman
  • Deathroot usage explanation
  • Dead-related quest guidance

Location:

Main chamber or standing watch

Quest Hook:

His storyline involves a journey to the Bestial Sanctum.

Fia

Deathbed Companion Blessings & Support

A mysterious woman who offers companionship to the Tarnished. She provides unique blessings and represents the theme of mortality and embrace.

Services:

  • Offers an embrace (gives temporary debuff or blessing)
  • Baldachin's Blessing - converts debuff to buff
  • Death blight sorcery

Location:

Bedroom chamber - resting area

Lore Connection:

Her character explores themes of death, dying, and the nature of Destined Death.

Enia

Finger Reader Great Rune Exchanging

A Finger Reader who becomes available after defeating your first demigod. She facilitates the exchange of Great Runes for valuable items and access.

Services:

  • Exchanges Great Runes for items
  • Late-game content access
  • Boss reward trading

Location:

Chamber of the Two Fingers - sacred space

Requirement:

Available only after defeating at least one Demigod and obtaining a Great Rune.

Diallos

Noble Questgiver

A well-dressed noble seeking a loyal servant. He has his own questline involving loyalty and ambition within the world of Elden Ring.

Services:

  • Quest initiation
  • Companion recruitment opportunities
  • Story progression

Location:

Common areas or private chambers

Character Note:

His quest involves seeking a loyal servant and has connections to other characters.

Notable Encounters

Ensha

Silent Guardian

A silent NPC standing outside Gideon Ofnir's room. In late-game, he may become hostile. He holds items of significance to certain characters.

Mad Tongue Alberich

Invader

A challenging invader encountered in the lower section of the Roundtable Hold. Defeating him grants the Taunter's Tongue and Reverential Bow gesture.
