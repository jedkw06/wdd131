Welcome to the Roundtable Hold

The Roundtable Hold is a sanctuary outside the Lands Between. It serves as the primary hub for Tarnished in the game Elden Ring. Combat is prohibited here.

Equipement Upgrades

Blacksmith Hewg enhances your Weapons up to +25 or +10. Roderika can upgrade Spirit Ashes to +10.

Npcs

Twin Maiden Husks offer a wide range of general goods, their inventory can be expanded through collecting Bell Bearings. Other merchants sell more targeted items for certain classes

Knowledge

Gideon Ofnir provides information about Shardbearers/Demi-Gods and their locations.
